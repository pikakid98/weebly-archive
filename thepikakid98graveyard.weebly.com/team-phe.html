Team PHE was intended to be a joint effort between Pikakid98 Studios (My company), Not-So Classic Games (Formerly Hand In The Cookie Jar Productions) and EyeShellGames. The PHE (pronounces fee) in Team PHE stood for Pikakid98 Studios, Hand In The Cookie Jar And EyeShellGames. Team PHE would have been the parent company while the other 3 companies would be subsidiaries. Team PHE would handle the publishing and everything else. This one lasted way too long with multiple Team PHE based websites being made as well as a YouTube channel, a Twitter account and a Facebook page. I even stupidly rebranded my Pikakid98 Studios Twitter to Team PHE.. and then deleted it and made a new one..... DOOFUS!!..
